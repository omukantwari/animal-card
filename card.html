Peacock

They cannot prolong their flight but these small drifts are extremely essential in getting rid of deadly killers.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo Cristatus
  • Average Length: 9 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Dense wooded area

The male Indian Peacock has iridescent coloring of blue and green on its head, neck and breast, their long ‘trains’ are not the tail quill feathers but elongated upper tail feather coverts. The train is bronze and green and has a series of ‘eyes’ which are more prominent when the tail is fanned.
